# Power and Industrial Electronics Lab (EEE-4110)

This repository contains MATLAB/Simulink-based simulations and analyses of various power and industrial electronics experiments conducted during my undergraduate studies at Pabna University of Science and Technology (PUST).

## Course Information
- **Course Title:** Sessional on Power and Industrial Electronics  
- **Course Code:** EEE-4110  
- **Institution:** Pabna University of Science and Technology (PUST)  
- **Semester:** 4th Year, 1st Semester  

## Experiments Included
1. Simulation of Diode Circuits (RC, RL, LC, RLC)
2. Single & Three-Phase Rectifiers
3. Single-Phase Inverters (Half & Full Bridge)
4. Buck, Boost, and Buck-Boost Converters
5. AC Voltage Controller with Resistive Load

## Tools & Technologies
- MATLAB/Simulink
- Power Electronics Components
- Circuit Modeling and Simulation
